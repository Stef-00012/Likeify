Likeify Privacy Policy

When you login with Spotify, Likeify only saves the following information:

  • Your Spotify User ID.
  • Your Spotify Display Name.
  • The ID of the playlist that was just created by Likeify.

When you logout, your Likeify account gets disabled, meaning your playlist won't be synced anymore.
If you want to completely delete your ID, Name and Playlist ID from Likeify, please send a mail to privacy@liked.spotify.stefdp.lol asking to do so.
